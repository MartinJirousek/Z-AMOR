Welcome

Welcome to the online resources for the course Anatomy and Morphology of Plants. This website provides learning materials that support understanding of the structure, development, and diversity of plants.

The content currently includes:

  • an overview of the course and main topics,
  • a dedicated page with study materials and topic descriptions,
  • a section with online revision tests.

Additional materials will be continuously added, including illustrations, images, downloadable files, and links to recommended videos. Each topic will be gradually expanded according to the content covered in lectures and practical classes.
